Meet your teacher

Alvaro Molina

Alvaro Molina is a self-taught Software Architect who currently works for Nordcloud supporting large enterprises migrate their workflow from on-prem to public cloud. He got into programming at the age of 12 by creating his own video games and by the age of 16 he launched his first successful video game with more than 1,000,000 players. After selling his company he’s been able to land job positions as a Software Architect, Senior Software Engineer and a Full Stack Engineer. Alvaro is also the Founder of Moodeling, a modeling social network available both on iOS and Android platforms.
